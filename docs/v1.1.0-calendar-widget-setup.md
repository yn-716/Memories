## Xcode Signing & Capabilities

Before App Store submission, verify these items in Xcode:

- The `Memories` app target has App Groups enabled.
- The `MemoriesWidgetExtension` target has App Groups enabled.
- Both targets include `group.com.myfs716.Memories`.
- The app target uses `Memories/Memories.entitlements`.
- The widget target uses `MemoriesWidgetExtension/MemoriesWidgetExtension.entitlements`.
- The URL scheme `memories` is present on the app target.
- The app target uses:
  - `PRODUCT_BUNDLE_IDENTIFIER = com.myfs716.Memories`
  - `MARKETING_VERSION = 1.1.0`
  - `CURRENT_PROJECT_VERSION = 3`
- The widget target uses:
  - `PRODUCT_BUNDLE_IDENTIFIER = com.myfs716.Memories.MemoriesWidgetExtension`
  - `MARKETING_VERSION = 1.1.0`
  - `CURRENT_PROJECT_VERSION = 3`

## Manual Device Checks

- Add the Home Screen widget in `systemSmall`, `systemMedium`, and `systemLarge`.
- Confirm Lock Screen widgets are not offered for Memories Pet Life.
- Register today's calendar photo in the app and confirm the small widget updates to today's image.
- Register only a weather icon and confirm it appears in the app calendar, calendar export, and widgets.
- Change a weather icon after changing its color and confirm the color is preserved.
- Confirm the medium widget shows one week, not a vertically compressed month.
- Confirm the large widget shows the month calendar.
- Confirm widget watermarks show the app icon plus `Memories Pet Life` for free users.
- Confirm widget watermarks are hidden for users with fresh unlimited access.
- Tap the small widget and confirm the app opens today's calendar flow.
- Tap the medium or large widget and confirm the app opens the Pet Calendar.
- Confirm Release builds do not show DEBUG-only controls.

## Notes

- Widget data is read from the App Group container only.
- The widget reads only `PetCalendarWidget/widget-snapshot.json` and pre-rendered widget JPGs from the App Group.
- App calendar images, thumbnails, and `index.json` stay in Application Support.
- The widget does not access the photo library and does not consume watermark-free export allowance.
- Widget snapshot regeneration keeps today and tomorrow image sets and prunes obsolete `pet-calendar-widget-*.jpg` files.
