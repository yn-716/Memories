# Memories Pet Life v1.1.0 Calendar / Widget Setup

## Xcode Signing & Capabilities

Before App Store submission, verify these items in Xcode:

- The `Memories` app target has App Groups enabled.
- The `MemoriesWidgetExtension` target has App Groups enabled.
- Both targets include `group.com.myfs716.Memories`.
- The app target uses `Memories/Memories.entitlements`.
- The widget target uses `MemoriesWidgetExtension/MemoriesWidgetExtension.entitlements`.
- The URL scheme `memories` is present on the app target.
- The app target and widget target both use:
  - `MARKETING_VERSION = 1.1.0`
  - `CURRENT_PROJECT_VERSION = 1`

## Manual Device Checks

- Add the Home Screen widget in `systemSmall`, `systemMedium`, and `systemLarge`.
- Add the Lock Screen widget in `accessoryInline`, `accessoryCircular`, and `accessoryRectangular`.
- Register today's calendar photo in the app and confirm the widget updates.
- Tap a widget and confirm the app opens the Pet Calendar.
- Tap the small/today widget and confirm the app opens today's calendar flow.
- Confirm Release builds do not show DEBUG-only controls.

## Notes

- Widget data is read from the App Group container only.
- The widget reads `PetCalendar/Widget/pet-calendar-widget-snapshot.json` plus thumbnails from `PetCalendar/Thumbnails`.
- The widget does not access the photo library and does not consume watermark-free export allowance.
